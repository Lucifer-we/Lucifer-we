About This Project

This website aims to recommend food videos based on your taste preferences. Whether you love sweet, salty, spicy, or sour food, this site will suggest videos to match your cravings. I have worked hard to curate a collection of videos that cater to various taste buds, providing you with a fun and personalized experience.

Throughout this project, I have learned web development, video curation, and the importance of user experience. This project is my effort to combine my passion for food with my skills in technology.
